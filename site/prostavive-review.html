ProstaVive is a prostate health supplement formulated specifically for men over 40 who experience the urinary discomfort, reduced flow strength, or frequent nighttime bathroom trips associated with an aging prostate.

How Does It Work?

ProstaVive targets the three primary drivers of prostate enlargement and dysfunction simultaneously:

1. DHT Inhibition — Saw Palmetto inhibits 5-alpha reductase, the enzyme that converts testosterone to DHT (the principal driver of prostate tissue growth).

2. Inflammation Reduction — Beta-Sitosterol reduces prostatic inflammation through prostaglandin pathway inhibition, demonstrating measurable improvements in urinary flow rates.

3. Hormonal Balance & Stress — Tongkat Ali and Ashwagandha address stress-hormones. Elevated cortisol suppresses testosterone production and worsens systemic inflammation.

What Is CelluCare?

CelluCare is a plant-based dietary supplement designed for adults who want to maintain healthy blood sugar levels and improve overall metabolic function. Unlike single-ingredient products, it combines over a dozen botanical extracts, each targeting a specific mechanism in glucose regulation, inflammation reduction, or energy support.

The product is manufactured in a GMP-certified US facility — meaning production meets federal standards for quality control. The formula is stimulant-free and contains no synthetic additives.

Important CelluCare is a dietary supplement, not a drug. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, or cure any medical condition. Consult your physician if you manage blood sugar with prescription medication.

How Does It Work?

CelluCare targets blood sugar through three primary pathways:

1. Glucose Absorption Modulation — Gymnema Sylvestre and Banaba Leaf slow the rate at which glucose enters the bloodstream after meals, reducing post-meal spikes.

2. Insulin Sensitivity Support — Corosolic acid in Banaba Leaf acts as a mild insulin mimetic, supporting cellular glucose uptake. This is the most studied mechanism in the formula.

3. Metabolic Inflammation Reduction — Turmeric Rhizome (curcumin) and Pine Bark Extract address chronic low-grade inflammation, a well-documented driver of insulin resistance.

What Is SleepLean?

SleepLean is a dual-action nighttime dietary supplement designed for adults who want to improve sleep quality while supporting healthy metabolism and body composition.

Its formula combines well-established sleep-support compounds — melatonin, GABA, ashwagandha, and L-theanine — with metabolic support ingredients like berberine and spirulina blue that work during the body's overnight repair cycle. It targets the deep physiological connection between quality sleep and effective fat metabolism.

Note SleepLean is not a magic weight loss pill. Results are most meaningful when combined with a reasonable diet and physical activity, using the improved sleep architecture to support metabolic health.

How Does It Work?

SleepLean works on two simultaneous tracks:

1. The Sleep Layer — Melatonin, GABA, 5-HTP, L-theanine, valerian root, and chamomile address sleep onset latency, sleep continuity, and sleep depth. When sleep improves, so does the hormonal regulation of hunger (ghrelin and leptin), reducing appetite dysregulation.

2. The Metabolic Layer — Berberine and spirulina blue activate AMPK pathways that support overnight glucose regulation and cellular energy expenditure, helping the body use stored fuel more efficiently during deep sleep phases.

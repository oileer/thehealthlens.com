The formula works through three primary pathways.

First — microcirculation support. Ginkgo Biloba improves blood flow to the cochlea — the spiral sensory organ of the inner ear. Reduced cochlear circulation is one of the primary triggers of both age-related hearing decline and tinnitus. This is the most studied botanical for this purpose.

Second — nerve and enzyme function. Zinc, Magnesium, and B12 support the nerve conduction and enzyme function that allow inner ear hair cells to accurately convert sound vibrations into neural signals. Deficiency in these minerals is disproportionately common among adults with tinnitus.

And third — antioxidant defense. Vitamins C and E provide protection against the free radical damage that accumulates in cochlear tissue over decades of sound exposure.
